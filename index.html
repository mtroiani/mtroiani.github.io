About Me:

Hi, I’m Mary Troiani. I’m a self-taught data scientist with a passion for machine learning and artificial intelligence. I love learning and always strive for continuous improvement. I’m willing (and happy) to put in the effort to master new skills.

I used to work in quality assurance and document control, where I was always the “go-to” person to find out what was wrong with our document control software. I enjoyed figuring out what was wrong and how to fix it, but I've always wanted to apply my training in cognitive science to tough problems, so I quit my job and learned to code. Now I love taking what I've learned about the brain to apply it to deep learning and other machine learning algorithms.
